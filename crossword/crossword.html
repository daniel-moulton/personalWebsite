Help

Welcome to my crossword game!

To get started, choose a category from the top and start playing!

Click on a cell to start typing there or click on a hint to start typing that word.

If you make a mistake either backspace until you get to it or use the arrow keys for easy navigation.

Once finished, click check to check your answers, or solve to autocomplete the entire grid!

Use the clear button to reset the grid and start again.

Click the settings cog to customise the size of the grid and how many words are in the grid!

Have fun!
